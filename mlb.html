AROUND THE GRAPEFRUIT & CACTUS LEAGUES
Spring training is in full swing. Here's what's happening across both leagues as teams prepare for Opening Day on March 26.
Spring Training Storylines
Dodgers Spring Preview
NL West Favorites
▾

The Los Angeles Dodgers enter 2026 as the heavy favorites in the National League West after their 2025 World Series title. Shohei Ohtani is fully healthy and pitching AND hitting this spring — his two-way capability makes LA arguably the most talented roster in baseball history. Freddie Freeman, Mookie Betts, and Will Smith anchor a lineup that ranked #1 in runs scored last season. The rotation is deep with Tyler Glasnow and Yoshinobu Yamamoto both projected healthy. Expect LA to contend for back-to-back titles.

Yankees Reload
AL East Contenders
▾

New York made several key acquisitions this offseason and enters spring training with genuine World Series aspirations. Juan Soto is locked in as the centerpiece of their lineup after his massive extension. The rotation question marks from 2025 appear addressed with a new front-of-the-rotation arm. Manager Aaron Boone enters the season with a roster that looks built to go deep in October. The AL East will be competitive with Baltimore and Boston both showing strong rosters.

Detroit Tigers: Year 3
AL Central Favorites
▾

The Detroit Tigers are entering Year 3 of their rebuild and this is the year expectations shift to contention. Tarik Skubal, their ace, is coming off a Cy Young season and looks like one of the best pitchers in baseball. Kerry Carpenter and Riley Greene give them a young core in the lineup. Detroit added some veteran pieces this winter and the AL Central — without a clear dominant team — is ripe for the taking. The Tigers are the team to watch in the Central.

Opening Day Preview
March 26, 2026
▾

Opening Day 2026 is March 26. All 30 teams will be in action on the first Thursday of the season. The marquee matchup features the defending champion Dodgers hosting the rival Giants in Los Angeles, while the Yankees will host the Red Sox in an AL East showdown. Other notable matchups: Cubs at Cardinals, Braves at Phillies, and the Astros at the Mariners in a rematch of last year's ALCS. Check back after Opening Day for live game recaps and standings.
