2025 NFL Season

SEASON
RECAP

The 2025 season delivered shocking upsets, a dominant Super Bowl, and the end of the Chiefs dynasty.

🏆
Super Bowl LX Champion
SEATTLE SEAHAWKS · 29–13 OVER NEW ENGLAND
Sam Darnold led Seattle to a 14-3 record and the NFC's top seed. The Seahawks dismantled the Patriots at Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara.
Key Story Lines
📉 Chiefs Dynasty Ends
KANSAS CITY FALLS TO 6-11
After three straight Super Bowls (2022–2024), the Chiefs had a stunning collapse in 2025, finishing 6-11 and out of the playoffs entirely. Patrick Mahomes faced an unprecedented rebuild year as key pieces departed in free agency. It was the most dramatic fall from grace the NFL has seen in a decade.
🦅 Eagles Defend LIX Title
PHILADELPHIA GOES 13-4, FALLS IN DIVISIONAL
The defending Super Bowl LIX champions led by Jalen Hurts and Saquon Barkley finished 13-4 and the NFC's third seed. Their postseason run ended in the Divisional Round against the eventual champion Seahawks in a closely contested game. Barkley continued his historic form and Hurts showed he's an elite franchise QB.
🚀 Sam Darnold's Redemption
FROM BUST TO SUPER BOWL CHAMPION
The most remarkable story of 2025: Sam Darnold, once seen as one of the biggest QB busts in NFL history, completed his redemption arc by leading the Seattle Seahawks to a Super Bowl championship. After a breakout 2024 season with Minnesota, Darnold signed with Seattle and never looked back — finishing the year as Super Bowl LX MVP.
🐉 Drake Maye Arrives
NEW ENGLAND PATRIOTS GO 14-3
Second-year quarterback Drake Maye proved he is the real deal, leading the Patriots to a shocking 14-3 season and all the way to Super Bowl LX. New England's rise was one of the most surprising stories of 2025, with Maye showing poise and big-game ability far beyond his experience level.
